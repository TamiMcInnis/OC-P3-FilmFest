ABOUT

THE FESTIVAL

The Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival is a fun event for the whole family...and completely free! Now a regular summer event, the festival is in it's fourth year running, and is made possible by the generous support from both the Bourough of Brooklyn, and the Brooklyn Bridge Park. With a selection of some of the best family films, the Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival is a great place to bring friends and family together. Grab a blanket and some popcorn, and join us for a fun night out under the summer stars!

DETAILS

DATE AND TIME

The festival opens on Thursday August 5 and runs until Sunday August 8. Each night the first film will start at 6pm, and the last film will finish by 12am. It is recommended that you arrive 30 min prior to the start of your film, to allow time to get through check-in and find a spot to settle down.

ADMISSION

Admission to this event is completely free! You may come for as many films as you wish to see, and we highly recommend registering for them in advance. Prority for admission will be given to those on the registration list. You are however, welcome to come without registering. Any available or unclaimed spaces will then be given out on a first come first serve basis at the check-in desk, 10 minutes prior to the beginning of each film.

ADDITIONAL INFO

A few tips to help you have a great time at the festival:

It is recommended that you bring folding chairs or a lawn blanket, as seating is not provided.
There will be concessions available in the park, but you are welcome to bring your own snacks and drinks as well.
Due to limited space, pets are not allowed at the festival.

LOCATION

BROOKLYN BRIDGE PARK

334 Furman St, Brooklyn NY, 11201 USA

The park entrance is located off of Furman St, and the check-in desk for the festival will be visible from there.

The park is easily accessible from Brooklyn Bridge Stn, NYC Ferry and Citi Bike Pier 2.
